Reddit Picture Gallery Downloader is installed and ready. Open any Reddit gallery and you'll see the Download button waiting in the corner.

Crucial browser setup

To prevent clicking "Save" for every single image, go to chrome://settings/downloads and turn OFF the toggle for "Ask where to save each file before downloading".

Customize your naming conventions

Click the "Customize Naming" button to open the builder. You can define exact dynamic filename rules for individual images, folders, and ZIP archives using drag-and-drop variables.

Sharing via Discord or Email?

Click the extension's puzzle icon in your toolbar and change your download mode to ZIP. Bundle entire galleries into one single file to share them effortlessly. 🤘
